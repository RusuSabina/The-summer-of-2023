Food and Drinks

During the summer you can find a lot of fruits and vegetables. Some of the richest from a nutritional point of view are cherries, wild berries, apricots and peaches, and in terms of vegetables, eggplant, corn, tomatoes and lettuce are valuable. It is important that the diet is varied, made up of easily digestible products such as fruits and vegetables, essential sources of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants or proteins.
